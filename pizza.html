Pizza Recipe

Easy to follow steps for the perfect and delicious Pizza

Ingredients

  • All-purpose flour: 2 cups
  • Mozzarella cheese: 1 cup, shredded
  • Bell peppers: 1 cup, sliced
  • Onions: 1 cup, sliced
  • Active dry yeast: 2 tsp
  • Sugar: 1 tsp
  • Salt: 1 tsp
  • Olive oil: 2 tbsp
  • Warm water: 3/4 cup
  • Mushrooms: 1 cup, sliced
  • Black olives: 1/2 cup, sliced
  • Dried oregano: 1 tsp
  • Black pepper: To taste
  • Fresh basil leaves: For garnish
  • Garlic: 2 cloves, minced
  • Cornmeal: For dusting
  • Extra olive oil: For brushing
  • Garam Masala: 1 tsp

Procedure

  1. Dough Preparation: In a bowl, mix warm water, sugar, and yeast. Let it sit for 10 minutes until frothy. Combine flour and salt, then add the yeast mixture and olive oil. Knead until smooth.
  2. Rising: Cover the dough with a damp cloth and let it rise in a warm place for about 1 hour or until doubled in size.
  3. Preheat Oven: Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C) while the dough rises.
  4. Prepare Toppings: Slice the vegetables and set them aside.
  5. Roll the Dough: After rising, punch down the dough and roll it out on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  6. Assemble Pizza: Place the rolled dough on a pizza stone or baking sheet. Spread pizza sauce, sprinkle mozzarella cheese, and arrange the vegetables on top. Add dried oregano and black pepper.
  7. Bake: Bake the pizza for 15-20 minutes until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbling.
  8. Serve: Remove from the oven, garnish with fresh basil leaves, slice, and serve hot.
