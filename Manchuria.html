Manchurian Recipe

INGREDIENTS

  • Corn flour 1/2 cup
  • Maida flour 1/2 cup
  • Spring onion
  • Soy sauce
  • Minced garlic
  • Ground pepper
  • Salt & Coriander
  • Onions: 2, chopped
  • Onions: 2, chopped
  • Tomatoes: 2, chopped
  • Green Chilies: 3-4, slit
  • Ginger-Garlic Paste: 2 tbsp
  • Turmeric Powder: 1/2 tsp
  • Red Chili Powder: 2 tsp
  • Coriander Powder: 1 tbsp
  • Cumin Powder: 1 tsp
  • Garam Masala: 1 tsp
  • Coriander Leaves: chopped
  • Salt: To taste
  • Oil: 3 tbsp
  • Water: 1 cup

PROCEDURE

S.No Description
1 Veggies Paste:Take all the veggies, finely chopped, in a bowl along with ginger, garlic, corn flour, maida, salt, black pepper and soy sauce.
2 Mixing: Mix them well, adding water as required for consistency.
3 Making balls: Make round balls out of the thick vegetable mixture.
4 Deep fry: Now deep fry the vegetable balls in a pan.
5 Prepare Sauce:Heat some oil in a pan and roast ginger, garlic and green chilli in it for a while Add spring onion, tomato ketchup, chilly sauce, soya sauce and vinegar followed by salt and black pepper.
6 Mixing:Mix them well and add a mixture of corn flour plus water to the pan.Mix thoroughly and put the fried vegetable balls into the mixture.
7 Serve:Properly mix the balls along with the sauce.Garnish with spring onion and celery. Serve hot along with cooked rice.
