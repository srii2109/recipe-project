Manchurian Recipe

Easy to follow steps for the perfect Veg Manchuria

Ingredients

  • Corn flour: 1/2 cup
  • Maida flour: 1/2 cup
  • Spring onion
  • Soy sauce
  • Minced garlic
  • Coriander Leaves: chopped
  • Salt: To taste
  • Ground pepper
  • Salt & Coriander
  • Onions: 2, chopped
  • Tomatoes: 2, chopped
  • Green Chilies: 3-4, slit
  • Oil: 3 tbsp
  • Water: 1 cup
  • Ginger-Garlic Paste: 2 tbsp
  • Turmeric Powder: 1/2 tsp
  • Red Chili Powder: 2 tsp
  • Coriander Powder: 1 tbsp
  • Cumin Powder: 1 tsp
  • Garam Masala: 1 tsp
